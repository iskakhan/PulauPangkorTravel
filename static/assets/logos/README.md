# Sponsor Logos / Logo Penaja

Place your 4 sponsor logo files here:

- `sponsor1.png` — Sponsor / Penaja 1
- `sponsor2.png` — Sponsor / Penaja 2
- `sponsor3.png` — Sponsor / Penaja 3
- `sponsor4.png` — Sponsor / Penaja 4

## Guidelines / Panduan

- **Format**: PNG or SVG (transparent background recommended / latar belakang telus disyorkan)
- **Size**: All logos will be displayed at the same size (68×44px display size) / Semua logo akan dipaparkan dalam saiz yang sama
- **Style**: Logos will be displayed white (filter applied) on the pink background / Logo akan dipaparkan putih di atas latar belakang pink

## How to update / Cara kemaskini

Simply replace the placeholder files with your actual logo files. The filenames must match exactly: `sponsor1.png`, `sponsor2.png`, `sponsor3.png`, `sponsor4.png`.

If you want to keep the original colours of the logo, remove the `filter: brightness(0) invert(1)` rule from `login.css` for `.sponsor-logo-item img`.
